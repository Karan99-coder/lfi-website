Overview

Linda Future Initiative (LFI) is a youth-led nonprofit organization based in Kenya, founded in 2023 to respond to the growing need for holistic youth empowerment. We tackle some of the most pressing challenges facing adolescents and young adults‚Äîincluding teenage pregnancies, early marriages, school dropouts, mental health crises, and climate change. Through transformative education, mentorship, and youth innovation, LFI empowers the next generation of leaders to shape their future and communities.

Since our founding, we‚Äôve impacted over 3,000 youths across Nairobi and Kiambu counties through more than 10 training sessions and outreach campaigns. Our programs emphasize mindset change, life skills development, and sustainable, community-driven solutions.

Vision

To become the leading youth empowerment hub in Kenya, equipping young people with the knowledge, tools, and values to thrive socially, economically, and environmentally.

Mission

To build a center of excellence that transforms the lives of adolescents and youth by fostering positive mindsets, leadership, innovation, and skills development across all dimensions of life.

Our Strategic Pillars

  • Social Impact & Entrepreneurship: Business skills, financial literacy, social innovation, and mentorship to launch income-generating youth initiatives.
  • Leadership & Personal Development: Emotional intelligence, values formation, and community mentorship.
  • Social Justice & Environmental Sustainability: Campaigns on gender-based violence, FGM, child marriage, and climate education.
  • Technology & Innovation: Digital literacy programs and innovation labs for creative problem-solving.

Flagship Programs

  • SRHR: Peer-led education on contraception, teen pregnancy, safe sex, and SGBV.
  • Mentorship & Mindset Transformation: Leadership, emotional well-being, and career coaching.
  • Creative Economy & Talent Development: Skills training in digital design, art, agribusiness, and online marketing.

Impact Snapshot

  • 3,000+ youth reached since 2023
  • 10+ trainings and outreach campaigns held
  • Youth-led projects launched in urban and peri-urban areas
  • Increased awareness in SRHR and mental health through peer-led platforms

Where We Work

Currently operating in Nairobi and Kiambu Counties with plans to expand nationally.
